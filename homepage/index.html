About Dogs for new Owners

If you're here, you've probably already gotten yourself a Dog or you're thinking about it.

Dogs can be awesome, loving, loyal and terrifically fun pets. But they require care and responsibility. This website is meant to get you started on the right path to raise a dog, but by no means is it comprehensive.

Here you'll learn about the generalities of dog, good caretaking practices, and get access to links to resources from very experienced dog owners that can tell you more about how to be a stellar pet owner. See the links in the navigation bar at the top of the page to get started learning!
